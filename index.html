I am a Melbourne-based User Experience and Front-End Designer with more than 15 years experience across various industries. I live on the line between design and development, with UX and Front-End being the perfect synergy for my skills. I am currently employed at the Suncorp Group as a Senior UX Designer working on personal insurance sites and web applications for some of the largest insurance brands in Australia. I am the founder of Suncorp’s front-end multi-brand styleguide.

Employment history

Senior UX Designer – Suncorp Group 2012 to current

  • Pioneer and creator of the Suncorp Web Styleguide - a living multi-brand front-end framework. This supported Suncorp in implementing Australia’s first responsive insurance quote flow and permits scaling of application design never before possible.
  • Sole specialist in component based and responsive design, HTML prototyping, progressive enhancement, icon curation, font-end mult-brand architecture and design for reusable patterns.
  • Design, development, information architecture, user testing and UX research for the Suncorp Insurance brands: AAMI, GIO, Suncorp, Bingle, IMR, Just Car, Terry Sheer, Apia quote flow applications for home, car and motorcycle insurance.

Internet Team Leader – The Just Group 2006 to 2012

  • 2006 to 2012 Manager of The Just Group Web Design team responsible for website maintenance and EDM campaign to hundreds of thousands of customers each week across 7 brands.
  • Implemented front-end integration for company-wide rollout of WebSphere Commerce, including site wide prototypes and dynamic marketing - Jay Jays and Dotti (in 2010), Portmans and Jacqui E (in 2011), Smiggle Peter and Alexander (in 2012).
  • Primary interface designer and front end developer for The Just Group websites, with builds including Just Jeans (2006 and again in 2009), Dotti (2007), Jacqui E (2007), The Just Group (2007), Smiggle (2008) and Peter Alexander (2008 to 2009).
  • Overhaul of The Just Group eCommerce platform front-end on the Interworld platform. Comprehensive work on customer registration, online product catalogues and checkout systems.
  • Animation and game design. Pioneered technique for Progressive Enhanced Flash site for Dotti.
  • Management, mentoring and training of junior and senior web designers. Project and team time management under continual heavy deadlines.

Lead Web Designer – ANZ Bank (anz.com) 2004 to 2005

  • Full responsibility for ANZ website graphics (anz.com), including homepage, promotions, banners, landing pages, animations and games.
